French Composition

----

State University of New York 2024

This 300-level undergraduate writing course involved introducing students to different writing styles. Through the semester, students learned to write for different purposes, from description to argumentation. It also aided students to discover connecteurs for agreeing, arguing, opposing, and taking a stand in a debate. At the end of the semester, students chose a topic and wrote a five-page essay on said topic.
